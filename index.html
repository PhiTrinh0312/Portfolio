Monitoring system for coffee plants showing information on Firebase and control by phone

Description

An automatic irrigation system for coffee plants is capable of controlling water irrigation and misting based on temperature and humidity levels. The collected data is sent to Firebase for monitoring and analysis.

This system features a mobile app (MIT app) for tracking and has two modes: manual (controlling the pump and misting machine through buttons on the phone) and automatic (automatically misting or irrigating when temperature or humidity falls below the allowed threshold).
